## âœ… Task Management

Clarity uses a system inspired by Notion but simplified for focus.

### Creating a Task
1. Navigate to **Tasks**.
2. Click **+ New Task**.
3. Enter a title. You can also add:
   - **Priority**: High priorities glow softly to catch your eye.
   - **Due Date**: For time-sensitive items.

> **Tip**: Break down large tasks into smaller steps using the **Subtasks** feature to reduce overwhelm.

### Organizing
- **Drag and Drop**: Reorder tasks to prioritize your day visually.
- **Status**: move items from "Todo" to "In Progress" to "Done".

## ðŸŒ¿ Habit Tracking

Build consistency without the pressure of "perfect" streaks.

### Setting Up a Habit
1. Go to **Habits** sidebar item.
2. Select **Create Habit**.
3. Choose a frequency (Daily, Weekly) and a color that resonates with the habit (e.g., Green for health, Blue for focus).

### Tracking
- Click the checkbox on your dashboard to mark a habit as done for today.
- **Streaks** are tracked to show your consistency, but we encourage focusing on the *trends* rather than fear of breaking a chain.

## ðŸ““ Journaling & Reflection

The heart of Clarity is the connection with yourself.

### Writing an Entry
1. Click **Journal**.
2. Start a new entry.
3. Use the rich text editor to format your thoughts.
   - Type `/` to open the command menu (add headers, lists, quotes).

### Mood Tracking
- Select your current mood (e.g., "Calm", "Anxious", "Focus") for each entry.
- Over time, Clarity can help you spot patterns relationships between your habits and your mood.
